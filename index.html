MoMA

Based on this Guerrilla Girls poster, we decided to investigate the gender distribution of artists exhibited at major museums. We asked the question "Is there a lack female representation at theMoMA?" From this, stemmed a larger question of what communities are over- and underrepresented at the Museum of Modern Art in NYC.

To answer this, we looked at a Kaggle dataset of all artworks exhibited by the MoMA up to approximately 2017 accompanied by various other metadata. We were interested in understanding representation of different communities at the museum.

Below, our exploration of this topic. Enjoy.
