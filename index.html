MoMA

For our semester project, we chose a dataset that contains various information on the MoMA artists from Kaggle. We were interested in understanding representation of females in the museum. In the past two centuries, we have known that female representation was very low compared to male representation. However, we wanted to explore the gender and cultural distribution of artists exhibited at major museums, using the MoMA as a case study.
