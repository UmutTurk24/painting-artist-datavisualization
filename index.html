For our semester project, we chose a dataset that contains various information on the MoMA artists from dataset. We were interested in understanding representation of females in the museum. In the past two centuries, we have known that female representation was very low compared to male representation. However, we wanted to explore the gender and cultural distribution of artists exhibited at major museums, using the MoMA as a case study.

Below, you can find each assignment we have completed so far.

Assignment 1: Bar-Chart

This assignment contains two barcharts regarding the number of male and female artist representations where both graphics tell a different story regarding the disparities between the genders

Assignment 2: Time-Series

Second assignment focuses on seeing trends for male and female representations over the years, as this was easily represented with a line-graph.
